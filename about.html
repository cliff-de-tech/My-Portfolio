Designer → Engineer

A creative technologist who bridges the gap between pixel-perfect design and production-grade code.

I Don't Just Write Backend Code.

I build APIs that Frontend developers love to consume — because I speak their language.

My journey started in visual design: crafting logos, marketing materials, and UI mockups. But I quickly realized that building the user experience everyone sees was only half the story. The real magic happens behind the curtain — in the data models, authentication flows, and distributed systems that power modern applications.

My Approach to Engineering:

  • Scalability First: Architecting systems that grow with your users, from 100 to 100,000+.
  • Security by Design: Zero-trust mindset, input validation, and proper authentication from day one.
  • User-Centric APIs: Clean endpoints, clear error messages, and documentation devs actually enjoy reading.

What I Bring to the Table:

  • Backend Architecture — RESTful APIs, microservices, authentication systems, and database design.
  • DevSecOps — CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code, containerization, and security automation.
  • Fintech Systems — Ledger design, payment processing, and PCI-compliant transaction handling.
  • Design Intuition — Years of UI/UX experience means I understand the frontend perspective deeply.
